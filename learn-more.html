Equipment

Smart Equipment Tracking

Never lose a piece of kit again. Use any phone camera to scan barcodes and instantly update location, quantity, and availability. Real-time dashboards show your entire inventory at a glance.

  • Barcode & QR scanning from any device
  • Live location and status updates
  • Quantity management across multiple stores
  • Equipment history and audit logs
  • Bulk import via CSV or spreadsheet
Scheduling

Season-Wide Schedule View

Visualise your entire production calendar in one place. Two-way Google Calendar sync ensures your team always has the latest schedule - on their phones, in their inbox, wherever they are.

  • Full-season timeline with all productions
  • Google Calendar two-way sync
  • Smart conflict detection
  • Filter by venue, crew member, or production
  • Export to PDF or iCal
Team

Crew Scheduling & Notifications

Assign roles, manage availability, and keep everyone on the same page with automated reminders. No more chasing crew members - ShowWise does it for you.

  • Role-based crew assignments

  • Rocket.Chat integration for instant comms
